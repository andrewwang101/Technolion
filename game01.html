To Infinity and Beyond

Welcome to our first mission, “to infinity and beyond”. In this game, your first challenge is to identify the perfect delta angle for a rocket to land on either the Mars or the Earth. To complete the challenge, you need some complicated formulas. Don’t worry, the calculation is not hard as you think, but you eject the rocket in a correct angle. Any calculation error will result in launch failure, so you need to check whether your answer is correct. Good Luck!
